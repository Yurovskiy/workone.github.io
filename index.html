Welcome to IGBeauty Studio

IGBeauty is a premium Cosmetology and Laser Clinic in Toronto. IGBeauty offering a complete collection of effective cosmetic, therapeutic and laser treatments, providing solutions for skin conditions and aesthetic enhancements. Our total experience in the skin care, healthcare and wellness industry is over 35 years. Our team includes a medical doctor, a medical esthetician, a laser technician, a massage therapist, a nail artist, nail technician, a makeup and a permanent makeup artist. At IGBeauty, we dedicate our attention to education. All our staff are fully licensed and take courses and train constantly to keep us in the leading edge on the newest technology, techniques and products. At IGBeauty, we have found the perfect harmony between nature and the latest technologies to achieve the most effective results.

Our services include: Natural skin care, Facials, Acne Treatments, Deep Cleansing Facial, Natural and Chemical Peels, Jet Peel, Oxygen Facial, Laser hair removal, Botox and Derma Fillers, Microdermabrasion, Anti-Aging Treatment, Skin Rejuvenation, Waxing, Micro Needling Therapy, Nail Care (manicure & pedicure, apparatus (Gehwol) pedicure and manicure), Massage Therapy (Aroma massage, Hot Stone massage), Sugaring, Collagen Induction Therapy, Scar Reduction Therapy, Skin Tag Treatment, Ruby Points Treatment, Telangectasia Treatment, Stellar angioma Treatment, Milia Removal, Cholesterol Deposit Removal, Non surgical face lift, Fine Lines and Wrinkle Reduction, Skin Tightening, Scars Removal, Facial Skin Rejuvenation, Jaw Lin Contouring, Turkey Skin Neck Tightening, Double Chin Reduction, Under Eye Bags Reduce, Cellulite Reduction, Fat Burning, Body Contouring & Firming, Stretch Mark Reduction, Pigmentation Removal treatments.

We proudly carry the best equipment and skin care products on the market, namely LightSheer Duet Laser, Pollogen Maximus with TriPollar & TriFractional Technology, eDermastamp, Dermaroller, Biophora, Yon Ka cosmetics, Holy Land Cosmetics, Christina Cosmetics, Rhonda Allison Cosmetics, Gehwol, Skin Management System by Dr. Straus, Natural Indulgence, Laura Natural Soap and many other brands. We invite you to come in for a custom profiling and personalized consultation at no charge.
